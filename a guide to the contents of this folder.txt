A guide to the contents of this folder

`archive`:		Old scripts and data.
`documentation`:	Analysis plan, technical reference, draft write-up, fonts and colors.
`figs`:			Exported figures.
`final`:		Exported datasets.
`interim`:		.dta files used with Longitudinal Tract Database.
`maps`:			Exported maps and associated .mxd.
`raw`:			Raw datasets.
